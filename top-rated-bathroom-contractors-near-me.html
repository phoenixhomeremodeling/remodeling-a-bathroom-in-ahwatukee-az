How much does it cost to tile a shower?

The cost of tiling a shower, encompassing tiles, installation, and related materials, generally amounts to approximately $25 per square foot, resulting in an average total cost ranging from $450 to $1,000, with the final expense contingent on the tile selection and the shower's dimensions.

Can you renovate a bathroom in 2 weeks?

In ideal conditions, a small complete bathroom remodel typically takes approximately 23 days, equivalent to roughly 4 1/2 weeks or slightly over a month if work is conducted continuously without weekend breaks.

Is it cheaper to tile a shower or buy a surround?

Custom-tile showers tend to be significantly more expensive than prefabricated shower units due to the need for skilled labor, although high-end prefabricated showers at the luxury level come close in terms of cost.
