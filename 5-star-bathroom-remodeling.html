Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a 5x8 bathroom remodel take?

The duration of most bathroom remodels typically ranges from 3 to 8 weeks, with simpler pull-and-replace or half-bath remodels leaning towards the shorter end, while fully custom master baths often necessitate the full 6 weeks or more, depending on the scope of work.

What color bathroom increases home value?

The choice of a light blue or periwinkle bathroom color scheme has the potential to increase a home's resale value significantly, by as much as $5,440 according to a study, whereas an off-white or eggshell bathroom can potentially decrease the value by several thousand dollars.

What is the best type of tile to use in a shower?

Porcelain tile and ceramic tile are the top choices for durability in a frequently used shower, as they are low-maintenance, easy to clean, and do not necessitate sealing, unlike more porous materials such as marble or other natural stones.

Is 5x8 big enough for a bathroom?

A bathroom measuring 5 by 8 feet, though seemingly compact, is the most common floor plan that can accommodate a sink, a shower and tub, and a toilet; however, those starting from scratch may face layout challenges due to the confined space.

What color bathroom sells the most?

When selling a house, the best bathroom color choices, as per a 2021 Zillow analysis, are soothing light blue walls, which can potentially lead to buyers being willing to pay 1.6 percent more, while other favored options encompass neutral colors such as gray, off-white, or light yellow.
