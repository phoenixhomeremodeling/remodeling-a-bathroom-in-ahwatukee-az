Frequently Asked Questions

How long does bathroom remodel take?

The duration of a complete bathroom renovation can vary significantly, ranging from as little as 3-4 weeks to as long as 9-10 weeks or even more, depending on the specific project requirements and scope.

How long does tile last in a shower?

Ceramic or porcelain tiled showers can last approximately 60-80 years with proper yearly maintenance, including resealing the tiles annually and replacing the grouting every ten years or more if the shower experiences frequent use.

What is the best time of year to remodel a bathroom?

Consider planning your remodel during the spring or early summer when contractors may have slower schedules, potentially resulting in reduced pricing, as this period, between holidays and summer vacations, typically offers greater contractor availability and improved product availability.

How often should you renovate your bathroom?

According to LT Plumbing experts, it is advisable to update your bathroom every 7 to 10 years, aligning with the typical lifespan of bathroom products, which can deteriorate due to wear and tear over time.
