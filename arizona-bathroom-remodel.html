What is the rule of thumb for bathroom remodel?

According to Consumer Reports, a general guideline for total remodeling cost is to allocate 5%-10% of your home's value, implying that for a $200,000 home, the remodel is likely to cost between $10,000 and $20,000.

Are walk-in showers a good idea?

A walk-in shower offers a blend of immediate satisfaction and long-term utility, providing homeowners with increased floor space, enhanced lighting, easier maintenance, and a contemporary aesthetic.

What is a good budget for a small bathroom remodel?

The typical cost of a small bathroom remodel averages around $6,500, with a normal range spanning from $1,500 to $15,000 or higher, contingent upon the complexity of the project, as stated by Licensed General Contractor, Architexture Home Center, Inc.

What should I remodel first?

Experts commonly recommend prioritizing the remodeling of your kitchen or bathroom first, as these areas traditionally offer the wisest investment, with kitchens often having higher renovation costs but ultimately providing a superior return on investment that pays off in the long term.
